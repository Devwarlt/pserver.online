Legendary Forge

All forging is done at the Sor Forge. This can be found to the right of the spawn in the Nexus.


To forge a legendary put a Legendary Sor Crystal at the top and the Shard at the bottom and click Forge. Depending on the Shard you use it will give you a legendary from the table of that Shard. Certain items can also be upgraded using a Legendary Sor Crystal such as the Warped Worlds Staff. Put the item you are upgrading at the bottom and the crystal at the top. Alternatively, at a fee of 75,000 gold, you can put a shine and a legendary item craftable from a shard in order to re-roll it into any other craftable legendary item from a shard.


Sor Crystal

Sor Crystals are vital items when it comes to forging legendaries. In order to successfully construct a Sor Crystal, you will need Onrane and Sor Fragments.

Gathering Sor Fragments

1 to 3 Sor Fragments drop from most bosses that you will run across such as events, dungeon bosses, and Oryx. You obtain the most from Raids, and Alerts. 50 of these Sor Fragments can be used to construct a Regular Sor Crystal. You can do this by going to your tabs and clicking the diamond symbol as shown below.


Then, go to your Sor Crystal Construct button. You should double check if you have enough Sor Fragments ready to build your crystal by hovering over the Sor Crystal picture.


Once you have enough Sor Fragments, click the Construct button and it will send a Sor Crystal to your Gift Chest!

Sor Crystal Ascension

Now that you have your Sor Crystal, you can now use Onrane to upgrade it to a Legendary Sor Crystal. Double-click or shift-click the Sor Crystal as if you were using it. Then, a prompt will show and ask you if you ready to ascend your crystal for 20 onrane. If you have enough Onrane, it will change your Sor Crystal to a Legendary Sor Crystal which can be used to forge legendaries.

Lootbox

Lootboxes drop from events such as Ghost Ship and Cube God. Lootboxes rank from Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Lootboxes also drop from Raids as Elite Lootboxes.


Elite Lootboxes can give you Onrane, Gold, and Sor Fragment Caches as well as Shards which are used to forge legendaries.


Bronze, Silver, and Gold Lootboxes give you various tiered loot as well as untiered loot. Silver and Gold lootboxes also have a chance to give you shards as well but at a very small rate.


Premium lootboxes contain Premium Effect chips, which activate a premium effect on one of your characters and can only be opened with Kantos.

Lootboxes can be accessed and opened at the nexus using the button shown right below your rank.


Currencies

Onrane

Onrane is used to open Elite Lootboxes, Ascended Lootboxes, forge vials, Ascend Sor Crystals, buy legendary items from the Zol Shop or the Titan Shop and activate new Nodes and Marks. Raids are the best source of Onrane because they drop Onrane Caches which can give 2-10 Onrane and the final boss of the raid can drop an Ultimate Onrane Cache that gives 12-20 Onrane. Elite Lootboxes can also drop Onrane Caches and Ultimate Onrane Caches. Onrane drops from mid-game and beyond content such as: Crypt of the Illusionist, Catacombs, Alerts and Raids.

Kantos is the premium currency for Valor, meaning it can only be obtained through donating. You recieve 1200 kantos per $10 donated. These Kantos can only be used for opening Premium Lootboxes to get Premium Effect Chip which activates a premium effect on 1 character. To see these effects, you must have Particle Master enabled in the experimental options.

Gold is used to purchase items from the Marketplace, it is the main currency of the server for trading with, it can also be used for buying backpacks, character slots and vault chests. Gold drops from mid-game content and beyond such as: Crypt of the Illusionist, Catacombs, and Raids.

Fame

Custom Stats

The Custom Stats are as follow:

  • Restoration (RES): restoration increases the amount of healing given when using health and mana potions. In addittion, having higher amounts of restoration causes you to automatically refil up to 6 of each potion upon visiting the nexus.
  • Protection (PRT): protection is a Shield which will protect you from all damaging projectiles, It can only soak as much as what it says. So if you have 131 it will protect you from 131 damage. This shield is not affected by defense.
  • Might (MGT): might is critical strike damage. You gain 0.1 bonus critical strike damage per 7 points.
  • Luck (LUC): luck is your critical hit chance. You gain 1% critical chance per 10 luck, capping at 100% critical chance at 1000 luck.
  • Surge (SG): surge is gained by killing enemies. You gain 2 surge per enemy that is killed near you. If you reach 100 surge, your protection bar is refilled. Depending on equipped items, surge can also have additional effects. Surge will naturally decay when enemies have not recently been killed.
  • Fortune (only given by items): this will increase your drop chance by %1 per stat point.

Boss: Ganus the Sincryer


Sincryer's Gate holds the first boss of the Zol Awakening Raid, Ganus the Sincryer. There aren't any complicated mechanics to this boss fight and it's pretty straight forward. To activate the boss you need to kill the 4 Stones of Zol surrounding the boss.

Behavior

Phase 1
Once the boss is activated he will go into a chase phase and shoot out 3 white shuriken shots that paralyze you and deal 200+ damage. Remember that you can take the first of these shots with your protection and not get paralyzed, but after your protection has been taken, you should focus on dodging. If you have a Shrine Maiden that can weaken enemies or give Armoured to everyone, you should stay in the group. If one is not in your group, it is recommended that you run up or down while shooting the boss to make sure you don't get paralyzed and sat on.

Phase 2
After paralyze phase the boss will run away from the player until he is stuck on a wall. He then shoots out a huge barrage of bullets that can insta pop a player. Since these shots do not pierce through players and can be tanked, it is recommended that you stay in a group in this phase if you are a squishy character.

Phase 3
After a short period of time the boss goes into phase 3. In this phase he will go invulnerable and go to the middle, spawning Zol Bombers all around the room. These Zol Bombers will throw out Zol Mines, which can be easily dodged as you can watch them throw them and make sure to stand out of the way. Be careful when killing Zol Bombers, because if you don't instantly kill them then they will turn red and try to dive on you, insta popping your character.

Phase 4
This phase is the worst in the fight: he starts off with a high damage barrage of bullets, similar to Phase 2, then becomes more passive and occasionally shoots out low damage piercing bullets. Soon after this he will start spawning in 2 Big Purple Slimes. It is advised NOT to kill these until after he activates tiles. A few seconds after the slimes spawn he will say something in caps in the chat. You will need to immediately get off of the dark blue tiles as they will become active and instantly kill your character. In this phase you want to now kill the slimes as them paralyzing you wont be a big deal as you are safe anyway.

Phase 5
After the tiles are turned off the boss will spawn little purple Zol Orbs. These will shoot out high damaging quiet bullets doing around 200 a hit, so you should focus these immediately. Shortly after that, if the boss hasn't been killed he loops back into Phase 1.

Boss: Aldragine


The combination of 1000 damage shots with the timed DPS phases while fallen foes become corrupted by the Zol makes this area the most difficult and dangerous. This boss fight requires communication and many players with end game gear to defeat Aldragine and banish the power of the Zol. This boss fight has many different phases to fight through and earns it's place as the final fight of the raid.

Behavior

Phase 1
To activate Aldragine you have to stand on him after he has turned green, when you stand on him he will turn red and he will be activated. Shortly after activating him he will start shooting.The first set of shots is 1 giant grey spinning bullet that deals 1000 damage but moves slowly along with a 4 shot 120 damage shot gun and 8 fast moving green shots shot in every direction that deal low damage but quiet. He will keep shooting these out until you have done enough damage to push him to the next phase, if you take too long he will shoot 4 green spinning shots in each corner that deal 240 damage as well as armor breaking and bleeding. After you do enough damage he will run to the side and begin Phase 2.

Phase 2
This is a minion spawning phase, he will spawn in 4 Captains of the Dark then shortly after 4 Zol Sorcerers, 2 Demons of the Dark and 2 Giant cubes of zol. He will stand still at the side shooting a burst of 1000 damage grey bullets. To push him out of the phase you need to kill the 4 Captains of the Dark, he CAN move into next phase with the other minions still alive if you just kill the captains so it's recommended to kill them last while focusing the other minions.

Phase 3
He then moves back to the center of the room. In this phase he shoots out 5 purple shots at the nearest player, dealing 120 damage each as well as shooting a faster moving green shot that quiets the player and a burst of black shots that slow the player. He does this for a short period before moving into Phase 4.

Phase 4
This phase is simple yet the only time you can fail this boss fight. He will spawn in 4 Stones of Zol on each side of the map, you have to kill these in a time frame or the boss will be too overcharged and leave the fight, giving you no loot or elite lootboxes. These Stones can't move but they shoot out some high damaging shots so be careful standing too close to them. After killing the Stones he moves into Phase 5.

Phase 5
He starts roaming a little bit around the middle, he never goes too far but be aware that he can move. He starts shooting out a chain of 4 green spinning shots in each corner that deal 240 damage as well as armor breaking and bleeding, there is no gaps in between the shots so the players are all separated into 4 segments, during this phase he also shoots out the 1000 damage grey shots at the nearest player, he shoots this a lot faster than before but they can still easily be dodged. After a short amount of time he will move into Phase 6.

He will throw out 3 Corrupted Stone Giants before going into Phase 7.

The Kraken

Tip

Always check how much air you have so you dont suffocate.

Behavior

When the boss has started he will start to shoot small low damage bullets randomly in front of him, these are of little threat to a geared character. While these shots are still being shot out the tentacles start to shoot out paralyze projectiles, these must be avoided as soon after this the tentacles will shoot out 4x200 damage bombs and you wont be able to dodge them, if you got paralyzed you will be hit by 2 bursts of these and most likely die. At the point of the second set of bombs being thrown the boss will probably have sunk back underwater. When he does this you need to go to the other side of the map to fight it again with the same pattern, you need to do this for all 4 sides.

After you have killed the boss on all 4 sides he will appear in the middle in a pool of water, he will spawn in kraken tentacles that you have to kill to be able to hit the boss. During this phase he will also throw out Giant Squids from the Ocean Trench, you should focus these as they will cover the the map with ink if they are left alone. After all the tentacles have been killed the boss will go vulnerable and armor broken, this will normally be where you kill the boss.

Drops of Interest

Item

Sor Fragment Caches, Sor Fragments, Onrane Cache and Onrane.

The Hollows


Hand of Terradius

Tip

Look out for any bombs as they do big damage and confuse.

Behavior

The boss will start with a chase phase, he will shoot out 8 swords in front of him as well as blue shots that stun in a burst all around him, this phase is very easy if you just circle him around the room. As the phase goes on for longer he loses the blue shot burst and the 8 swords become 4 swords. After that phase he will gain a new cone attack in front of him, this time with blue balls that paralyze the player, he does not perfectly follow you in this phase and free roams around with an occasional dash.

After this he will change to shooting 3 blue shots in front of him, these are slow moving so you will never get hit by them if you circle, while he's doing this he also shoots out blue wave shots that do not apply a status effect. After this he runs back to the middle for a damage phase followed by a stationary turret phase where he targets the player and shoots directly at him. He stays in this stationary turret phase until he starts healing then he goes back into a rage chase phase where he shoots out paralyze shots and tries to sit on you.

Drops of Interest

Item

Sor Fragment Caches, Sor Fragments, Onrane Cache and Onrane.
