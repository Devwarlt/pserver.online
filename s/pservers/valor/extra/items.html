A core aspect of the game is finding and collecting items that will enhance your character in various ways. There is plenty of loot to collect in the Realm, and plenty of monsters to take it from. Naturally, more desirable loot comes from increasingly difficult areas of the game, but everything can be utilized in one way or another.
