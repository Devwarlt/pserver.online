Valor is an RotMG-based private server with extra challenges to overcome.

Play as one of the 21 classes available in this unique experience, team up with your friends to fight challenging bosses, explore new custom dungeons and conquer the realm.

Will you slay Sidon or will you be yet another to get corrupted by the power of the Zol? Take the fight to Aldragine in The Zol Awakening raid or try and seal Drannol the Eternal Beast once again in a fight for the safety of the realm in the Calling of the Titan raid.

Game Features
  • Raids Custom dungeons with 4 bosses, each more difficult and more rewarding than the last
  • Auto realm close system
  • Ascended system
  • Forging system
  • Lootbox system
  • Marketplace system
  • Marks system
  • No pets
  • Custom items
  • Custom realm events
  • Custom stats
  • New currencies: Onrane and Kantos
  • New tiers: Fabled, Legendary and Ancient Relic
  • New base stats
  • New classes
  • Team Work fame boost
  • Arena with wave based rewards and exclusive contents
  • Special commands for donator: /glow, /reskin and /size
